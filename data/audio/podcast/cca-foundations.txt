LEO: And configuration, the CLAUDE.md files and hooks?

MAYA: Memory first. CLAUDE.md files form a hierarchy, a global one in your home directory's dot claude folder applying to every project, a project-level one for project-specific rules, and one at the repo root for architecture overview, and Claude Code reads them as persistent instructions.
